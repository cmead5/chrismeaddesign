Micro Meadows

Local farming meets brand strategy for a fast-growing microgreens company

Spotlight

Micro Meadows is a local company that uses vertical farming to grow high-quality microgreens and craft small-batch salad dressings. What began at farmers markets quickly evolved into a fast-moving brand with a loyal following—and a growing footprint across the Midwest.

I helped shape their brand from the ground up—starting with a redesigned logo and label system for their dressing line. I also directed the product photo shoot, ensuring the visuals reflected the freshness and care behind the brand. Everything from sales sheets and banners to market canopies and packaging came through my hands.
