DraftKings

Custom layout design for high-profile athlete promotions

Spotlight

I was brought in to design small-batch, custom layouts for promotional cards that extended national campaign work. While the base artwork came from New York, the layout and execution were mine—created fast and on-brand for local markets and event-specific drops.

Some of these layouts were personalized for major names like LeBron James, Shaquille O’Neal, and Kevin Hart. The final designs were literally in their hands.

We gave the decks big energy—color, contrast, pacing. I designed branded iconography, background treatments, and templated transitions that gave each section a distinct feel. I also built alternate formats and style guides for their partners and internal reps.
